# Customer Churn Analysis Project Documentation

## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most important business metrics for subscription-based companies.

This project analyzes customer churn behavior using SQL to identify retention patterns, customer segments, and revenue loss.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations lose revenue when customers discontinue services.

The business wants to understand:

* Why customers leave.
* Which customer groups are at risk.
* Which regions experience the highest churn.
* How much revenue is lost due to churn.

---

## Project Objectives

* Calculate churn rate.
* Identify churn by region.
* Analyze churn by contract type.
* Evaluate payment methods.
* Calculate revenue loss.
* Segment customers by age.

## Business Insights

* Month-to-Month contracts tend to have higher churn.
* Certain regions show higher customer loss.
* Revenue impact can be measured directly through churn analysis.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to analyze customer retention patterns and support business decision-making.
